THE WITH FRIENDS
MAIL CLUB

Not a newsletter. A monthly delivery of real, physical things made by people in our community — for people in our community.

Each box might include
  • Original artwork printed on postcards by our community artists
  • Stickers designed by members of the club
  • Written work — poems, short prose, zine pages — printed on paper
  • Friendship coupons and small prompts to do in real life
  • A note from the community — what we made, who showed up

Who we are
ABOUT US

We started as a group of friends wanting to stay connected to our passions. Now we run art events, life skills workshops, and community clubs at cafés across Toronto.

Our story
LEARN AND CREATE

We believe that creativity and community aren't luxuries — they're life essentials. We started running art sessions at cafés because we missed making things with our hands, together, without pressure or performance.

Now we run Bad Artist Sessions, life skills workshops, and community clubs — all at cafés across Toronto. No experience needed at any of our events. Just show up, order a drink, and make something.

See Upcoming Events →
Our values
✳
No skill required, everWe design every event so that showing up is the only prerequisite.
✳
Community firstWe're not a service — we're a group of people who keep showing up for each other.
✳
Real life, in personEverything we do happens face to face, at a table, in a café, in Toronto.
✳
Support the spaceWe partner with local cafés — please purchase a drink when you attend.
