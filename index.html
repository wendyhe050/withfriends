About the community
LEARN AND
CREATE

We started as a group of friends wanting to stay connected to our passions. Now we run art events, life skills workshops, and community clubs at cafés across Toronto. No experience needed — just show up and make something.

A real thing in the mail
THE WITH FRIENDS
MAIL CLUB

Not a newsletter. A monthly delivery of real, physical things made by the people in our community — for the people in our community.

Each box might include
  • Original artwork printed on postcards by our community artists
  • Stickers designed by members of the club
  • Written work — poems, short prose, zine pages — printed on paper
  • Friendship coupons and small prompts to do in real life
  • A note from the community — what we made, who showed up
$12/mo
